Our Mission

Founded in 2018, ConfessionWeb's mission is to give users a centralized platform to join, connect and help each other. People use confessionweb to seek help and help others in need which eventually helps them to confess their thoughts, emotions and let them vent out the things confined inside them for a longer time.

Our Story

Change is everything and that's what we do here. Back then, people were just using their confessions to just confess but we wanted to make those confessions memorable and more authentic so that people in need can seek help and help others in return.
This is where we started. We found a way to make every confession special and people should not wander around to seek help or help others.

So we are building a most-productive, advanced and authentic confession-based system which will let our users to make their confessions more authentic and genuinely help others.
